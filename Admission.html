Imprest

The *Imprest deposit (to be replenished as and when required to maintain a base minimum amount) covers advance against expenses, viz. uniforms, books/ stationery/ art & craft material/ fine art material/ school publications, performing arts/photography and other clubs/ other hobbies, school photographs. Extra coaching, French classes, special learning classes, Model United Nations (MUN), IAYP, SUPW, counseling, workshops/seminars, book fairs, tennis, extra/out of school sports, mid-term/ other outings/ picnics, postage/ telephone, Xeroxing, Information Technology (IT), school functions, birthday, farewell/House parties, mattresses, bedcovers, toiletries, dry-cleaning, beautician, extra diet, pocket money /tuck shop, Late Fee, escorted homeward/ schoolward journey etc., is adjusted against Imprest Deposit. The *Imprest deposit that is to be maintained for each Indian student stands at INR 75,000 (with a minimum balance of INR 15,000 to be maintained at any given time). The *Imprest deposit for international students stands at USD 1500.
