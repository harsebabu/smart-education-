WELCOME TO DELHI PUBLIC SCHOOL SITAMARHI BIHAR

In the thriving age of girls boarding schools, Ecole Globale has manifested itself as the best girls boarding school in Dehradun, as well as in India. Located in the magnificent valley of Dehradun, the abundant and world-class campus of the school depicts our efforts in creating a supreme learning environment for young girls. Ecole Globale International Girls' School is furnished with all the latest technologies and amenities. We strive to improve women's representation and mold young girls into strong ladies with excellent leadership qualities. The girls here are prepared to master the art of professionalism and to host a cool house party. We believe in the amalgamation of distinct cultures and backgrounds. With girls from all over the country, we have made a diverse atmosphere where students are a part of the mature, smart, and intelligent community of women.
